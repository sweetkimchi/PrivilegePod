# AuditTrail - synthetic evidence corpus (example_file)

Fully **fictional** demo data for the AuditTrail product. Names, companies, dollar amounts,
and dates are invented; email addresses use the reserved `.example` TLD. Any resemblance to a
real person, agency, or case is coincidental. Generated by `make_examples.py`.

## The story (synthetic)
Meridian Healthcare Staffing LLC, a nurse-staffing contractor, was hired by the Westridge Department of Health Services (WDHS) under emergency contract
MSA-2021-114 during the 2021 statewide respiratory surge. Over six months Meridian billed
ten invoices. WDHS underpaid several of them in different ways - the largest being a $48,200
invoice it approved and then refused to pay. This corpus is the email + document trail.

## What's here
- `mailbox/meridian_inbox.mbox` - 34 emails with attachments embedded (feed THIS to AuditTrail).
- `mailbox/eml/` - the same emails as individual readable `.eml` files.
- `attachments/invoices/` - digital invoice PDFs (text layer) + the photographed scan
  `INV-2021-0447_SCAN.pdf` (image only, no text layer - triggers the scanned path).
- `attachments/receipts/` - `Lodging_Receipts_0425.pdf` (scanned, two lines struck in red).
- `attachments/screenshots/` - vendor-portal screenshots showing INV-0447 APPROVED, then DENIED.
- `attachments/spreadsheets/` - Meridian billed register, FEMA reimbursement summary, onboarding roster.
- `ledger/WDHS_Warrant_Register_2021.xlsx` - the payer's payment ledger (the "paid" side).
- `ground_truth/` - `reconciliation_key.csv` and `DISCREPANCIES.md` (the answer key).
